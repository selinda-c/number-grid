Prime Numbers

A number that is only divisible by 1 and itself is known as a prime number. Every prime number is an odd number except number 2.

Composite Numbers

A number that is divisible by 1, itself, and one or more other numbers is known as a composite number

Fun Fact!

1 is neither a prime nor a composite number.

How about Odd and Even numbers?

Odd numbers

Definition of odd number. : a whole number that is not able to be divided by two into two equal whole numbers The numbers 1, 3, 5, and 7 are odd numbers.

Even Numbers

Definition of even number. : a whole number that is able to be divided by two into two equal whole numbers The numbers 0, 2, 4, 6, and 8 are even numbers.
